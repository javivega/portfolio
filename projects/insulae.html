The challenge

In this project I discovered a two-sided problem. Landlords lose time in the management of rented properties, as some of them are not automated or domiciled, so they had to spend time remembering and checking the rent payment.

In addition, some tenants share a flat and do not want to pay the rent for their flatmates in advance.

Defining

After interviews and surveys, two user personas were developed in order to have a better understanding of our users.

In terms of strategy, I decided to approach the problems of the landlords the most, but taking alwais in consideration that, if the app didn't solve the problems of all the users, it would have not be successful.

I deepen on understanding and defining the main strucutre and possible functionalities taking into account the iformation found out on the firs stage of the design process.

Branding and look & feel

The insulaea were blocks of flats, usually rented, of several floors in Ancient Rome. They were used by citizens who could not afford private dwellings.

Insulae takes a look to the past, to reivent the present of the rental management.
