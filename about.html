Hi! I'm Javi Vega

I love learning. I'm a self-taught person with a background in design and economics. I studied Business Administration and Advertising in Madrid.

I love to travel and I am constantly looking for new experiences. I was born in Malaga, but I lived in Madrid, Warsaw and Tenerife.

I decided to specialice as a Product designer, so I studied the UX/UI bootcamp at Ironhack Madrid.

I am currently working at Mindset as a Graphic Designer. I help giving a concept and designing and building the visual identity of learning experiences for other companies.
